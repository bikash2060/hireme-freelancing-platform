Project Description

We are looking for an experienced full-stack developer to build a custom e-commerce platform for our boutique fashion brand. The platform should support product listings, shopping cart functionality, secure checkout, user accounts, and basic inventory management.

Key Requirements:

  • Responsive design that works on all devices
  • Integration with Stripe or PayPal for payments
  • User registration and login system
  • Product search and filtering
  • Admin dashboard for managing products and orders
  • Basic analytics to track sales and visitor behavior

Additional Information:

We already have a logo and brand colors established. The design should be clean, modern, and focus on showcasing our products effectively. We expect the developer to provide guidance on best practices for e-commerce UX.

The project should be completed within 12 weeks. We're open to suggestions on technology stack but prefer solutions that are maintainable and scalable.
