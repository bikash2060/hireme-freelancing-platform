Our Story

From humble beginnings to Nepal's leading freelance platform

2018

Founded in Kathmandu

Started by a team of developers and entrepreneurs who saw the potential of Nepal's growing tech talent but lacked a dedicated platform to connect with quality clients.

2019

First 100 Freelancers

Onboarded our first 100 verified freelancers across web development, design, and content creation categories.

2020

Pandemic Growth

As remote work surged globally, we helped 500+ Nepali professionals transition to freelance work during challenging times.

2022

NPR 1 Million Milestone

Our community collectively earned over NPR 1 million through the platform, proving the viability of freelance careers in Nepal.

Today

Leading Platform

Trusted by thousands of freelancers and clients worldwide with projects completed across 50+ categories.
