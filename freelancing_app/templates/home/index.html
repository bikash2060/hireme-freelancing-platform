Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with HireMe

What is HireMe and how does it work?

+ ×

HireMe is Nepal's premier freelancing platform that connects clients with verified Nepali freelancers for various projects. Clients can post jobs and receive proposals, while freelancers can browse projects and submit bids.

Is HireMe free to use?

+ ×

Yes! Clients can post projects for free, and freelancers can sign up and apply to jobs without any upfront cost. We also offer premium features for enhanced visibility and support.

How do I post a project?

+ ×

To post a project, simply log in to your client account, go to the "Post Project" page, fill out the required details like description, budget, and timeline, and publish it. Freelancers will start submitting proposals shortly.

How do freelancers get paid?

+ ×

Freelancers are paid through HireMe's secure escrow system. Clients deposit funds, which are released once the work is completed and approved.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work?

+ ×

If you're not satisfied, you can request revisions, pause the project, or initiate dispute resolution. We ensure that your payments are only released for approved work.

Can I work with international clients or freelancers?

+ ×

Yes. While we focus on the Nepali freelance market, we support both local and international collaborations, especially for remote projects.

Are freelancers on HireMe verified?

+ ×

Yes, all freelancers undergo a verification process based on skills, experience, and identity before getting access to apply for projects.

How do I communicate with freelancers/clients?

+ ×

You can use our in-platform real-time messaging system to chat, share files, and discuss project milestones securely.

Is my personal and payment information secure?

+ ×

Absolutely. We use advanced encryption and secure protocols to protect all user data, including payment and personal details.

Can I cancel a project after hiring a freelancer?

+ ×

Yes, you can cancel a project at any time. If funds are already in escrow, the cancellation will initiate a refund or dispute process based on the situation.
