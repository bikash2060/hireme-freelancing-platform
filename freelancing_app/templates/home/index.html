Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started as a freelancer?

Sign up, create a profile, and start bidding on projects that match your skills.

How can I find freelance work?

Browse available projects and submit proposals to clients.

How do I get paid for my freelance work?

Payments are processed securely through the platform once the work is completed and approved by the client.

Can I work with clients from other countries?

Yes, you can work with clients from anywhere in the world. The platform connects freelancers with clients globally.

Is there any fee for using the platform?

Our platform charges a small service fee when a job is completed and paid. Please refer to our pricing page for detailed information.

How do I update my profile?

You can update your profile details anytime through the profile settings page.

Can I work part-time on the platform?

Yes, you can choose part-time or full-time opportunities based on your preference.

What happens if I can't complete a project on time?

Communicate with your client as soon as possible. You may request an extension if needed.

How can I rate a freelancer's work?

You can rate the freelancer once the project is completed and approved by both parties.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our support team through the "Help" section on the website.
